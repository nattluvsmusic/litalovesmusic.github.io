About

I shoot,
write, and curate.

I'm Lita — a concert photographer, staff writer, and show curator embedded in the Boston and NYC music scenes. I spend my nights in photo pits and green rooms, my days writing about what I find there, and my weekends thinking about what should be on stage next.

My work lives at the intersection of the image, the story, and the room it all happens in. I'm interested in the moments between songs, the rituals before doors open, and the acts nobody's booked yet but everyone will be talking about.

Available for editorial licensing, artist shoots, commissioned writing, and show curation. Based in Boston, working Boston and NYC, willing to travel for the right project.
